Privacy Policy for Skipr

Skipr respects your privacy.

Skipr does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

All functionality of the extension runs locally within your browser. 
The extension only interacts with supported streaming websites (such as Netflix) to automatically click “Skip Intro” and “Next Episode” buttons.

Skipr does not:
• Track your browsing activity
• Collect personal information
• Store user data externally
• Share data with third parties

The only permission used is "storage", which is used solely to save user preferences (such as toggle settings) locally on your device.

If you choose to support the project via the “Buy Me a Coffee” link, you will be redirected to an external website. Any information provided there is handled by that platform and not by Skipr.

If you have any questions, you can contact the developer.

By using Skipr, you agree to this privacy policy.
